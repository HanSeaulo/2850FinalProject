# Job Stories

## Sung Jinwoo

Job Story 1:

When Sung is trying to book a flight in between meetings, 
he wants it to be quick and not take forever, 
so he can just get it done and move on.


Job Story 2:

When a meeting gets moved or cancelled, 
he wants to change his flight without having to redo everything, 
so it doesn’t waste his time.


Job Story 3:

When he is about to pay, 
he wants to clearly see the full price with baggage and any extra costs, 
so there are no random charges later.


Job Story 4:

When he books again, 
he wants his details already there, 
so he doesn’t have to type everything in every time.


Job Story 5:

When done with booking, 
he wants to download the invoice straight away, 
so he can send it for expenses.

## Maria

Job Story 1:

When Maria is searching for flights on a tight budget,
she wants to quickly find the cheapest options,
so she doesn’t spend too much time comparing.


Job Story 2:

When she is choosing a flight,
she wants to clearly see the full price including all extra fees,
so she doesn’t get surprised at checkout.


Job Story 3:

When browsing different flights,
she wants simple filters (price, time, duration),
so she can narrow down options easily.


Job Story 4:

When booking from her phone,
she wants the process to be fast and simple,
so she can complete it without getting frustrated.

## David 

Job Story 1:

When David is browsing flights with his poor eyesight,
he wants everything to be clearly visible and easy to read,
so he doesn’t struggle or miss important details.


Job Story 2:

When he is booking a flight with limited technical skills,
he wants a simple step-by-step process,
so he doesn’t get confused or make mistakes.


Job Story 3:

When he is choosing between different flights,
he wants to easily compare comfort and price,
so he can pick the best option for him and his wife.


Job Story 4:

When he is going through the booking process,
he wants it to have as few steps as possible,
so he doesn’t feel overwhelmed and give up.


Job Story 5:

When he is about to confirm the booking,
he wants clear summaries and confirmations,
so he feels confident everything is correct.

## Sophie 

Job Story 1:

When Sophie is managing multiple reservations,
she wants to see all bookings in a clear and organized way,
so she can quickly understand and track them 


Job Story 2:

When a customer requests a change,
she wants to modify bookings quickly and easily,
so she can handle requests without delays.


Job Story 3:

When there are cancellations or availability updates,
she wants to process them efficiently,
so the system remains accurate.


Job Story 4:

When she is working long hours on the system,
she wants a darker or less bright interface,
so her eyes don’t get strained.

When making changes to bookings,
she wants confirmation prompts and clear feedback,
so she avoids accidental errors.
