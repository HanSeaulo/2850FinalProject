**Agenda:** Product Owner Meeting  

## Summary

We met with the product owner and introduced ourselves properly as a team. We discussed our understanding of the Flight Booking system and asked several questions about scope, realism, security, reporting depth, and how modification/cancellation requests should be handled.

The product owner encouraged us to use our own judgement when making design and implementation decisions. She suggested looking at existing airline websites (e.g., Air China) as a reference for layout and structure.

Overall, the guidance was high-level, so most technical and structural decisions will need to be defined by our team.

We currently have initial personas, job stories, user stories created. These still need to be reviewed and finalised. We scheduled another product owner meeting for **6th March**.
