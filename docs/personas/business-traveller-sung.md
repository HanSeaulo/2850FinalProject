# Persona: Sung Jinwoo

## Context & Role

Sung Jinwoo, 38 years old. Sales manager.

- Travels two to three times a month for work and meetings across Europe.
- Usually books flights himself and needs flexibility for a fast and changing schedule.

---

## Pain Points

- Meetings often get rescheduled, so might need to change the flight last minute.
- Hates long booking processes, especially between meetings.
- Gets frustrated to re-enter details.
- Needs invoices for expense claims.

---

## Goals & Success

- Clearly see total price including baggage and extra costs before paying.
- Add multiple passengers easily.
- Be able to cancel or change flight.

---

## Constraints & Non-Goals

### Constraints:
- Often books from his phone while travelling.
- Limited time and attention.

### Non-goals:
- Does not want to call customer support unless absolutely necessary.
