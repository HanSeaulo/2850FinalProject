# Persona: Sophie

## Context & Role

Sophie, 26, works for the airline to manage the booking system.

---

## Pain Points

- Will spend many hours a day on the website.
- Prone to making mistakes.

---

## Goals & Success

- Be able to track reservations easily.
- If there are cancellations, availability changes or booking changes she is able to manage that easily.
- Be able to modify bookings quickly and easily.

---

## Constraints & Non-Goals

### Constraints:
- Eyestrain from white light may prevent her work.

### Non-goals:
- Doesn’t want to mess up a booking by making an error on the website.
