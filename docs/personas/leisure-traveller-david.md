# Persona: David

## Context & Role

David, 65, wants to book a flight to Germany for a holiday with his wife.

---

## Pain Points

- His eyesight is not very good.
- Not technically able.

---

## Goals & Success

- Wants to be able to evaluate options to pick between comfort and price.
- Wants the system to be easy to use because he is old and not good with tech.

---

## Constraints & Non-Goals

### Constraints:
- Poor vision.
- Poor technical ability.

### Non-goals:
- Will give up and find a different way to do it if the website is too difficult.
